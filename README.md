# Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive overview of global sales performance. It is designed to help stakeholders quickly identify high-performing regions and product categories while tracking essential KPIs.

## 🚀 Key Features
* **KPI Tracking:** Real-time visibility into Total Sales Amount (29.31M) and Total Order Quantity (91K).
* **Geospatial Analysis:** A regional map highlighting sales distribution across Europe, North America, and South America.
* **Categorical Breakdown:** A bar chart comparing sales performance across "Bikes," "Accessories," "Clothing," and "Components."
* **Interactive Slicers:** Users can filter the view by "Average Sales per customer," "Number of costumer," or "Total Sales."

## 🛠️ Tools Used
* **Power BI:** For data visualization and dashboard creation.
* **Bing Maps API:** For geospatial data rendering.
* **DAX:** Used for calculating custom measures like average sales and customer counts.

## 📈 Insights
* **Dominant Category:** Bikes represent the vast majority of sales revenue compared to other categories.
* **Global Reach:** The business maintains a strong presence in the Atlantic-facing regions (Europe and the Americas).
* **Volume:** With over 91,000 orders, the logistics chain is high-volume, supporting a total revenue of nearly 30M.
